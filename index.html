About Me

I'm Ada, a Data Scientist with a passion for uncovering insights from data and making data-driven decisions. Currently, I'm a Data Scientist at the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, where I do research on large language models. I'm also a calligrapher and classical violinist. Welcome to my personal website!

Why I Chose Data Science

Data science feels like a natural extension of who I am — it’s part of my personality!

  • Love for Organization: Data scientists need to organize their data first to make sense of it! My friends tell me "you're the most organized person I know."
  • Data-Driven Decision-Making: Every decision I make is rooted in logic and evidence, which is the heart of data science!
  • Attention to Detail: Just like a data scientist should validate their results, I naturally double-check everything, ensuring accuracy and reliability in my work.
